General Signage Questions

How do I choose the right signage for my business?

The right signage depends on your location, visibility needs, operating hours and brand positioning. For example, LED sign boards work best for retail businesses, while ACP hoardings suit large buildings and long-term branding.

Is the budget shown on the website final?

The budget estimate shown on our website is an approximate range based on size and material. Final pricing is confirmed after a site inspection to ensure accurate installation and safety.

LED Sign Board FAQs

Are LED sign boards suitable for outdoor use?

Yes. Outdoor LED sign boards are weather-resistant and designed to perform well in rain, heat and dust. They are ideal for shops located on main roads and high-traffic areas.

How long does an LED sign board last?

A well-maintained LED sign board typically lasts between 5 to 7 years. Power consumption is low, making it cost-effective in the long run.

ACP Hoarding FAQs

What is ACP and why is it popular?

ACP (Aluminium Composite Panel) is a lightweight yet durable material widely used for outdoor signage. It offers a clean finish, long lifespan and excellent resistance to weather.

Is ACP hoarding suitable for multi-storey buildings?

Yes. ACP is commonly used for commercial buildings, hospitals and showrooms due to its structural safety and premium appearance.

3D Letter Signage FAQs

Where is 3D letter signage best used?

3D letter signage is ideal for offices, clinics, hospitals and corporate spaces. It creates a premium look and helps build trust with customers.

Can 3D signage be customized?

Yes. 3D signage can be customized in terms of material, lighting type, size and color to match your brand identity.
