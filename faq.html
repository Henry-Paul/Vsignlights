Product & pricing questions

How is signage pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on size (square feet), material type, lighting requirements, installation height and location complexity.

Why do prices vary between LED boards and ACP hoardings?

LED boards involve lighting components and power supplies, while ACP hoardings focus more on panel strength and structure.

Do you provide custom sizes?

Yes. All signage is custom-manufactured based on your exact width, height and branding requirements.

Are the prices mentioned final?

Prices shown are indicative ranges. Final pricing is confirmed after site inspection and design finalisation.

Installation & service questions

Do you handle installation?

Yes. We provide end-to-end services including fabrication, transport and professional installation.

How long does installation take?

Installation timelines vary based on size and site conditions, but most projects are completed within 1–3 days.

Do you help with permissions?

We guide clients on local signage regulations and permission requirements wherever applicable.

Which locations do you serve?

We serve businesses across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.
